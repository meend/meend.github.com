Meend - a smooth, melodic glide between two notes, connecting them together. Meend brings two notes closer, just as mutually-loved music plays common friend to two strangers. Meend is an intricacy that connoisseurs love to discern, but it is also music beyond notations - a melody that cannot be technically transcribed - something you and I can appreciate for no other reason because it 'sounds pretty'. Meend is a connection, a bond between all music lovers. Be it prolific artistes, upcoming musicians, the myriad genres and trends in music or the fascinating musical instruments, the pages of Meend unite every musical entity. Meend invites every music lover to connect.
